Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory,Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Paris, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cared are flipped face up over each turn.The object of the game is
to turn over pairs of matching cards

Why Play Memory?!

Memory is a fun game for all ages, it can be played by yourself or with a group! Memory Game exercises the brain, making it more sharp and alert. If you play Memory Game at least thirty minutes every day, your concentration and focusing ability will improve.
